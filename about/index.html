About Me


I am a student at Embry-Riddle, Daytona. At a young age, when I was flown up in a Cirrus SR22 and allowed to steer it, I realized I wanted to dedicate myself to flying. By the time I was 18, I earned my PPL at the quaint Greenwood Lake Airport in New Jersey and went on to get my Commercial License a year later.

I enjoy making art, with much of my paintings and illustrations being of birds, B-2s, and other aircraft! I also like to game, watch classic movies, and seek thrills by going on roller coasters, hang gliding, or even skydiving.
